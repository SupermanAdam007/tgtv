Real-time microphone voice distortion. Start the mic, pick an effect, and hear your transformed voice through the TV speakers.

Use a USB microphone or the TV's built-in mic. To avoid feedback, lower the volume or keep the mic away from the speakers. With npm run log, events are tagged [voice-fx] in debug.log. The simulator may not provide audio input — test on a real TV for best results.
